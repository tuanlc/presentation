Project

  • It’s a service that will be used by Linagora to guarantee the functioning of open source components for customers.
  • Provided by engineers dedicated to handle and process the support requests of customers.
  • Allows service recipients to communicate with support teams by e-mail, telephone, fax, fixed and mobile internet, videoconference

Provides a management tool for:

  • Organization
  • Entity (Sub organization)
  • Contract
  • Order (Unit Of Work)
  • Softwares
  • Support request (ticket)
